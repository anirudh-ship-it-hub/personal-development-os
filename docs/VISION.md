# Vision

## What Level Up Is Not

Level Up is **not**:

- A habit tracker — habits are a means, not the product
- A productivity dashboard — no generic metrics wall or admin-style overview
- A project manager — no Gantt charts, sprints, or task backlogs

## What Level Up Is

**Level Up is a mobile-first RPG where the player becomes their future self.**

The player chooses a journey aligned with who they want to become. Each day they receive quests — small, actionable steps that compound over time. Completing quests earns XP, levels unlock new capabilities and better habits, and the player gradually transforms into the future self they defined at the start.

Gamification serves behavior change. Every screen should feel like playing a game, not managing a to-do list.

## Core Gameplay Loop

```
Choose Journey
        ↓
Receive Mission
        ↓
Complete Daily Quests
        ↓
Earn XP
        ↓
Level Up
        ↓
Unlock Better Habits
        ↓
Become Future Self
```

### Loop Breakdown

1. **Choose Journey** — The player selects or creates a long-term path (health, career, relationships, etc.) through the Mission Builder.
2. **Receive Mission** — AI generates a personalized mission with focus areas and quests tailored to the player's goal.
3. **Complete Daily Quests** — On the Execute screen, the player checks off today's quests across active journeys.
4. **Earn XP** — Each completed quest awards XP. Perfect days and streaks provide bonus rewards.
5. **Level Up** — XP accumulates into levels with rank titles (Explorer → Pathfinder → Builder → Strategist → Architect → Mastermind).
6. **Unlock Better Habits** — Higher levels and journey progress unlock more advanced quests and focus areas.
7. **Become Future Self** — Over weeks and months, consistent play closes the gap between current self and future self.

## Long-Term Vision

Level Up becomes the daily companion for personal transformation:

- **Personal RPG** — Every user has a Hero with level, rank, XP, streaks, and a visual identity that evolves over time.
- **Meaningful Journeys** — Multiple concurrent journeys (health, career, relationships) each with their own progress, focus areas, and quest lines.
- **AI Coach** — An intelligent guide that adapts missions, suggests quests, and celebrates milestones in character.
- **Social Layer** — Guilds, accountability partners, and shared achievements for players who want community.
- **Native Feel** — A PWA that installs on the home screen, sends push reminders, and feels like a native mobile app.
- **Behavior Science** — Streaks, perfect days, and XP are designed to reinforce identity change, not just checkbox completion.

The north star: **open the app each morning, see your quests, play your day, and watch yourself level up into who you're becoming.**
